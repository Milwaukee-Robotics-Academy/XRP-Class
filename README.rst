Introduction to Robotics with XRP

=================================

Welcome to the Introduction to Robotics course using XRP robotics! This course is designed to provide students with a hands-on learning experience in the exciting field of robotics. By leveraging the XRP platform, students will explore fundamental concepts in robotics, programming, and engineering design.

Throughout the course, students will:

- Learn the basics of robot construction and design.
- Program robots to perform tasks using logical and algorithmic thinking.
- Understand key concepts such as sensors, actuators, and control systems.
- Solve real-world problems through creative and iterative design processes.

The XRP robotics platform offers an accessible and engaging way to dive into robotics, making it suitable for beginners while still providing depth for advanced learners. By the end of the course, students will have gained valuable skills in problem-solving, teamwork, and computational thinking, preparing them for further exploration in STEM fields.
